Our Services

Data Analysis

“It is a capital mistake to theorize before one has data.”
- Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, Sherlock Holmes

At Aviation Consultancy LLC we believe in the idea that data quality is at the heart good business decisions. We take a detailed approach to the process of cleaning, transforming and modelling data to discover useful information for business decision-making. We aim to help you extract useful information from data, unlocking insights and taking decisions based upon the data analysis.

Data Migration

"Data is a precious thing and will last longer than the systems themselves" - Tim Berners-Lee

At Aviation Consultancy LLC we help you select, prepare, extract and transform your data, transferring it from your legacy system. We look to support the automation of as much of the migration as possible to free up valuable resources to keep your operation running during the project cycle. We support your business needs through speed, scalability and security of the migration process.

Data Integrity

“In God we trust, all others bring data.”
— W Edwards Deming

At Aviation Consultancy LLC we understand that the assurance of accuracy, completeness and consistency of the data is of the utmost importance and a critical aspect of the design of the data warehouse. Regulatory compliance - such as GDPR - data quality and data security are maintained by designing the rules, data standards and business process during the design phase.
